Our Story

From a simple idea to a global movement

2008

Inception

Little Light was founded by Sarah Johnson after witnessing the struggles of children in a remote village. She started with a small scholarship program for 5 children.

2012

Expansion

We expanded our programs to include healthcare access, reaching 500 children across 10 communities. Received our official nonprofit status.

2016

International Reach

Opened our first international operations in two countries. Launched our Safe Shelter program for homeless children.

2025

Today

Now serving over 25,000 children across 120 communities worldwide. Recognized as a leading child-focused charity organization.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our work

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged children by providing access to quality education, healthcare, nutrition, and safe environments, enabling them to break the cycle of poverty and achieve their full potential.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive, learn, and grow into a healthy, educated, and empowered adult who can contribute positively to society.
